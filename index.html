Welcome!

Sunderland have a long and illustrious history. Our 6 League Titles make us the 6th most successful team in English football, but our glory years were before the advent of live games or even highlights of games were available. Here, you can watch videos of Sunderland matches from the 1900’s to the 1990’s - including some of the classic games from Roker Park.
You can also see some of the star players of the past in action, and some of our greatest goals and moments.
Hopefully is brings back many memories and shows the great heritage of our club.
